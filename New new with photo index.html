About the Supplier and Mission

With a PhD in Honeybee Health, Dr. Haftom Gebremedhn combines deep scientific knowledge with more than 18 years of practical experience in apiculture. As a passionate honey supplier from [translate:Tigray], Ethiopia, he is dedicated to promoting 100% pure and natural honey sourced from the region’s diverse floral landscapes.

Over the years, Dr. Haftom has built a strong network with local beekeepers, providing hands-on coaching on how to harvest, handle, and store high-quality honey, as well as training on seasonal colony management. He has contributed to publishing more than 23 beekeeping research findings in reputable international journals (ORCID Profile) and has authored books and practical manuals written in local languages to support and educate beekeepers.

He has also received international training certificates in modern beekeeping from Ghent University (Belgium) and the Hebrew University of Jerusalem (Israel). In addition, Dr. Haftom actively supports academic development by advising and teaching MSc students in beekeeping and related fields.

His mission is to ensure that every drop of honey he supplies reflects the purity of [translate:Tigray]’s natural environment while supporting and empowering the rural beekeeping communities who make it possible.
